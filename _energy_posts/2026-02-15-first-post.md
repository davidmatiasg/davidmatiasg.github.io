Why do electricity prices differ across locations on the same grid? A quick intuition for **locational marginal prices (LMPs)**.

- Prices differ across nodes because of **congestion** and **losses**.
- LMPs help allocate scarce transmission capacity.
- They create incentives for generation and load to locate efficiently.
